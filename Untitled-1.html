    Crop Detection Technologies

    Modern precision agriculture leverages various AI and IoT technologies for crop detection and health monitoring:

    • Satellite Imagery: High-resolution images analyzed with AI to detect crop stress, disease, and growth patterns.
    • Drones: Equipped with multispectral and thermal cameras to capture detailed crop data for early problem detection.
    • Machine Learning Models: Image classification and object detection algorithms identify crop types, diseases, and pest infestations.
    • IoT Sensors: Soil and environmental sensors provide continuous data streams to complement visual crop health data.
    • Robotics: Automated systems use AI to perform targeted interventions like spraying or harvesting based on crop detection.
